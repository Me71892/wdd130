Lake Tahoe Campgrounds

One of my favorite lakes and camping spots in Nevada is Lake Tahoe. Lake Tahoe is a deep lake and over two million years old. It attracts many people from California and is a great ski attraction. The lake lies at 6,225 feet, which is 1.15 a mile. Lake Tahoe itself is 1,645 feet deep. The Truckee river flows out of Lake Tahoe northeast to Pyramid Lake. The water of Lake Tahoe has been tested for purity and is 99.994% pure. Eight campgrounds around Lake Tahoe are “D.L Bliss State Park Campground, Meeks Bay Resort and Marina, Meeks Bay Campground, Fallen Leaf Campground, Eagle Point Campground, Bayview Campground, Campgrounds at Camp Richardson Historic Resort and Marina, and Campground by the Lake”(Law). Come on down to the lake and camp!
